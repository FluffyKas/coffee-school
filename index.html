Coffee School

Coffee School is one of the industry's leading barista training centres, providing home coffee and "beyond coffee" education and experiences for anyone passionate about a good cup of espresso

Ready to become your favourite barista?

Barista Fundamentals

No experience needed, these courses will teach you the basic but essential skills from getting to know the equipment and the origin of coffee, all the way through pulling the perfect espresso.

Latte Art

The ultimate guide to milk foaming and creating incredible latte art.

Advanced Barista Training

Skill-based and SCA certified courses for home brewers and working baristas on best practices, brewing methods, sensory skills, etc.

Roast Training

Taught by the industry's experts, these courses offer you everything you need to know to become a roast master.

Beyond Coffee

Various buyer's guides, water chemistry, business planning and measuring your carbon footprint.

Online Training

Sign up and start learning: 9 premium online courses with over a 100 videos, providing the same standards as our traditional courses.
